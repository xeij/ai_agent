# Shipping and Returns Policy

## Shipping Information

### Shipping Methods and Costs

**Standard Shipping (5-7 business days)**
- FREE on orders $500+
- $19.99 on orders under $500
- Tracking provided

**Expedited Shipping (2-3 business days)**
- $49.99 flat rate
- Available for all products
- Tracking and insurance included

**Express Shipping (1-2 business days)**
- $99.99 flat rate
- Order by 2 PM EST for same-day processing
- Signature required

**Local Pickup**
- FREE
- Available at our warehouse locations
- Ready in 1-2 business days
- Bring photo ID and order confirmation

---

### Processing Time

**In-Stock Items:**
- Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship same business day
- Orders after 2 PM EST ship next business day

**Custom Builds:**
- Standard Build: 5-7 business days + shipping
- Premium Build: 7-10 business days + shipping
- Rush service available (+$199 for 2-3 day build)

**Pre-Orders:**
- Ship on release date or when stock arrives
- You'll receive email notification when shipped

---

### Shipping Restrictions

**We Ship To:**
- All 50 US states
- APO/FPO addresses (standard shipping only)
- US territories (additional fees may apply)

**We Do NOT Ship To:**
- PO Boxes (for complete systems; components OK)
- Freight forwarders
- International addresses (Canada and Mexico available with additional fees)

## Returns Policy

### 30-Day Return Window

**Standard Return Policy:**
- 30 days from delivery date
- Item must be in original condition
- Original packaging required
- All accessories and documentation included

**Restocking Fee:**
- Opened systems: 15% restocking fee
- Unopened systems: No restocking fee
- Components: 10% restocking fee (opened), 0% (unopened)
- Custom builds: 20% restocking fee

**No Restocking Fee If:**
- Item is defective (DOA)
- We shipped wrong item
- Item doesn't match description

---

### Return Process

1. **Request RMA**
   - Contact support: 1-800-SUPPORT or returns@smartsupport.com
   - Provide order number and reason for return
   - Receive RMA number (required for all returns)

2. **Prepare Package**
   - Include all original packaging, accessories, manuals
   - Include copy of invoice and RMA number
   - Do NOT write on original product box

3. **Ship Return**
   - Use provided prepaid label (for defective items)
   - Or ship at your expense (for buyer's remorse)
   - Insure package for full value
   - Keep tracking number

4. **Refund Processing**
   - Inspection upon receipt (2-3 business days)
   - Refund issued to original payment method
   - Refunds process in 5-7 business days
   - Restocking fee deducted if applicable

---

### Non-Returnable Items

**Cannot Be Returned:**
- Software (opened)
- Gift cards
- Downloadable products
- Custom-painted or engraved systems
- Items marked "Final Sale"

**Special Conditions:**
- Monitors: Must have 8+ dead pixels for return (manufacturer policy)
- Opened CPUs: Must be in original packaging with seal intact
- Thermal paste: Cannot be returned if opened

---

### Defective or Damaged Items

**Dead on Arrival (DOA):**
- Report within 7 days of delivery
- No restocking fee
- Free return shipping
- Replacement shipped immediately (advance replacement available)

**Damaged in Shipping:**
- Report within 48 hours of delivery
- Take photos of damage and packaging
- We'll file claim with carrier
- Replacement or refund processed immediately

---

### Exchanges

**How to Exchange:**
- Follow standard return process
- Indicate desired replacement item
- We'll ship replacement when return is received
- Or use advance exchange (credit card hold required)

**Price Differences:**
- Exchanging for more expensive item: Pay difference
- Exchanging for less expensive item: Receive refund of difference (minus restocking fee if applicable)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I return an opened product?**
A: Yes, but a restocking fee applies (10-20% depending on product type). Defective items have no restocking fee.

**Q: How long does it take to get my refund?**
A: 2-3 days for inspection after we receive the return, then 5-7 business days for refund to process to your account.

**Q: Who pays for return shipping?**
A: For defective items, we provide a prepaid label. For buyer's remorse, customer pays return shipping.

**Q: Can I return a custom build?**
A: Yes, within 30 days, but a 20% restocking fee applies unless the system is defective.

**Q: What if I'm missing the original packaging?**
A: Returns without original packaging may be subject to additional restocking fees or may not be accepted. Contact support to discuss options.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Yes, if it hasn't shipped yet. Contact us immediately. Once shipped, you'll need to follow the return process.

**Q: What if the product goes on sale after I buy it?**
A: We offer a 14-day price protection. If the price drops within 14 days of your purchase, we'll refund the difference.
