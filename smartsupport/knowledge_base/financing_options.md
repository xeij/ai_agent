### Important Terms

**No Interest if Paid in Full:**
- Pay off balance within promotional period = 0% interest
- No prepayment penalties
- Pay more than minimum to pay off faster

**After Promotional Period:**
- If balance remains after promo period ends, standard APR applies (currently 24.99%)
- Interest charged from original purchase date on remaining balance
- **We recommend:** Set up autopay to ensure timely payments

**Minimum Monthly Payment:**
- Calculated to pay off balance within promotional period
- Typically 1/12th of balance for 12-month term, etc.
- Must pay at least minimum to maintain 0% APR

## Alternative Financing Options

### Progressive Leasing (Lease-to-Own)

**For customers who don't qualify for traditional financing:**

**How It Works:**
- Lease the product with option to own
- Initial payment required (typically $49-$99)
- Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly payment options
- Own the product after completing payments

**Benefits:**
- No credit needed (different approval criteria)
- Fast approval process
- Flexible payment schedules

**Terms:**
- 12-month lease-to-own
- Early buyout options available (save money by paying off early)
- 90-day buyout: Pay only lease price + fees
- Higher total cost than 0% APR financing

**Example:**
- $1,500 gaming PC
- Initial payment: $99
- Monthly payments: $140 x 12 months
- Total cost: $1,779 (includes lease fees)
- 90-day buyout: $1,575

## Financing FAQs

**Q: Will applying for financing affect my credit score?**
A: The initial pre-qualification is a soft credit check and won't affect your score. If you proceed with financing, a hard inquiry will be made, which may have a small temporary impact.

**Q: Can I pay off my balance early?**
A: Yes! There are no prepayment penalties. Paying off early ensures you pay 0% interest.

**Q: What happens if I miss a payment?**
A: Late fees may apply, and you may lose the 0% APR promotional rate. Set up autopay to avoid missed payments.

**Q: Can I finance accessories and components?**
A: Yes, financing is available on all products, not just complete systems.

**Q: What if I return a financed item?**
A: Your financing balance will be adjusted to reflect the return. Refunds are applied to your financing account.

**Q: Can I have multiple financing accounts?**
A: Yes, you can have multiple active financing accounts up to your approved credit limit.

**Q: Is there a minimum purchase amount?**
A: Yes, $500 minimum for our 0% APR financing program.

**Q: Can I use financing with other promotions?**
A: Yes! Financing can be combined with most promotions, discounts, and trade-in credits.
