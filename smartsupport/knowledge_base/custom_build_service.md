## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I provide my own components?**
A: Yes! We charge a flat $199 assembly fee for customer-supplied parts. Note that warranty coverage will be limited to manufacturer warranties on those parts.

**Q: How long does a custom build take?**
A: Standard builds take 5-7 business days. Premium and Ultimate builds with custom work take 7-14 days.

**Q: Can I make changes after ordering?**
A: Yes, as long as we haven't started assembly. Once assembly begins, changes may incur additional fees.

**Q: Do you offer water cooling?**
A: Yes! We offer AIO liquid cooling (included in build fee) and custom loop water cooling (Ultimate Build tier or +$599 for custom loop service).

**Q: What if I'm not sure what components to choose?**
A: Our build specialists will guide you through the entire process and make recommendations based on your budget and use case.
