## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the difference between GeForce RTX and RTX professional cards?**
A: RTX professional cards (A-series) have optimized drivers for professional software, ISV certifications, larger VRAM, and enhanced stability. They're designed for 24/7 operation and come with professional support.

**Q: Do I need ECC memory?**
A: ECC memory is recommended for professional workflows where data integrity is critical, such as scientific computing, financial modeling, and professional content creation. It prevents data corruption and system crashes.

**Q: Can I upgrade components later?**
A: Yes! Our workstations are designed for easy upgrades. You can add more RAM, storage, or even upgrade the GPU. Upgrades don't void your warranty.

**Q: Do you offer custom configurations?**
